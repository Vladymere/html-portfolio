The Best Movies According to Vladymere

My top 3 War movies of all-time.

Gladiator

This is one of my favourite war movies where it signifies the courage and resilience of Maximus.

Troy

“Troy” is an epic retelling of the Trojan War, focusing on the legendary conflict, love, and betrayal that lead to the fall of Troy.

Hacksaw Ridge

“Hacksaw Ridge" tells the true story of Desmond Doss, a pacifist medic who bravely saved dozens of lives during World War II without carrying a weapon.
